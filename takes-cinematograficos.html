01

Como Adaptar

Use os prompts trocando apenas os elementos principais da cena.

Personagem principal

Character A

Substitua pelo personagem principal da cena.

Segundo personagem

Character B

Substitua pelo outro personagem que participa da ação.

Cenário

Location

Defina o ambiente onde a cena acontece.

Clima emocional

Mood

Informe o tom emocional da cena: tensão, intimidade, ameaça ou conflito.

Frase obrigatória para referências

Para usar com imagens de referência, mantenha sempre a frase: preserve the identity, face, outfit, body proportions and visual style from the reference images.

02

Takes para Conversa entre Dois Personagens

Planos para diálogo, reação, intimidade, confronto equilibrado e desequilíbrio de poder.

Take 01

Two-Shot / Plano de dois

Função: mostra os dois personagens no mesmo quadro. Serve para relação, tensão, parceria, intimidade ou confronto equilibrado.

Prompt 
Create a cinematic two-shot still frame of Character A and Character B in the same composition, both clearly visible inside the frame, facing each other in a tense conversation. Preserve the identity, face, outfit, body proportions and visual style from the reference images. Medium shot framing, eye-level camera, balanced composition, natural body language, readable facial expressions, subtle emotional tension between the characters. Use cinematic lighting, soft shadows, realistic depth, professional film still quality, ultra-detailed, RAW, HDR, no text, no watermark.
Take 02

Over-the-Shoulder Shot / Plano sobre o ombro

Função: coloca o espectador atrás de um personagem, focando no outro. Dá sensação de conversa real, ponto de vista e subtexto.

Prompt 
Create a cinematic over-the-shoulder shot from behind Character A, with Character A's shoulder and part of the head visible in the blurred foreground, while Character B is sharp in focus in the background, speaking or reacting during an intense conversation. Preserve the identity, face, outfit, body proportions and visual style from the reference images. Medium-close framing, 50mm to 85mm lens look, shallow depth of field, natural eyeline, emotional tension, cinematic lighting, realistic skin detail, soft background blur, professional movie still, ultra-sharp, RAW, HDR, no text, no watermark.
Take 03

over-the-shoulder shot

Função: destaca Character B como foco principal, com Character A desfocado no primeiro plano para criar profundidade, tensão e leitura emocional.

Prompt 
Using the attached reference images as CHARACTER_A and CHARACTER_B, preserve both identities, facial features, hairstyles, clothing, body proportions and overall visual consistency.

Create a classic over-the-shoulder cinematic scene in [LOCATION]. CHARACTER_A is positioned very close to the camera with their back turned to the viewer, occupying part of the foreground frame. CHARACTER_A must be heavily out of focus, showing only the blurred back of the head, shoulders and upper body silhouette.

CHARACTER_B is positioned in the second plane / midground, facing CHARACTER_A directly. CHARACTER_B must be perfectly sharp and clearly visible as the main subject of the image, with an expression that matches the mood of the scene. The camera focus must be locked on CHARACTER_B, not on CHARACTER_A.

Use a strong emotional atmosphere with [MOOD], clear depth separation, blurred foreground, sharp midground subject, cinematic framing, 85mm lens look, shallow depth of field, realistic lighting, detailed environment, polished character design, elegant visual quality, ultra-detailed textures, and a professional vertical cinematic composition.
